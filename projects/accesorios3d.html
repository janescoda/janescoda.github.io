01 · Overview

A minimal 3D printed brand for everyday interiors

Accesorios3d is a brand focused on designing and producing decorative pieces and home accessories through 3D printing, with an emphasis on minimal aesthetics, quality and sustainability. The goal is to offer contemporary objects that can blend into different interior styles without losing personality.

The proposal combines a refined, almost “luxury” look with accessible pricing, using sustainable materials and responsible production processes. The brand bets on durability and timelessness, prioritising clean forms, careful details and high‑quality finishes.

The website lets people customise certain products – from dimensions to colours or finishes – so pieces can adapt to the needs of each home. The purchase experience is designed to be clear, quick and pleasant, with efficient shipping and close, human customer support.

Responsibilities
  • Definition of the visual identity and brand positioning.
  • Design and layout of the e‑commerce website.
  • Product design, 3D printing and finishing of the pieces.
