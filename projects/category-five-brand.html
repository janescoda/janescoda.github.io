Digital communication

Category Five Brand

Digital campaign for Category Five Brand built around audiovisual pieces, photographic content and graphic assets designed for social media, with the aim of connecting with a young audience interested in streetwear, urban culture and contemporary trends.

Client
Category Five Brand
Industry
Fashion
Role
Art direction, graphic design and digital content
Year
2023

The campaign is built around three short videos that portray different summer activities connected to the brand’s universe: days at the beach, motorbike routes and urban events featuring skateboarding. Each piece is designed to highlight specific garments from the new collection and showcase their versatility in real-life contexts, aligning with the audience’s lifestyle.

In parallel, a digital campaign focused on Instagram and TikTok was developed, combining photographic content, graphic pieces and logo animations. The content plan integrated Stories, Reels and grid posts conceived both as a visual lookbook and as a showcase of the brand’s identity, maintaining a coherent aesthetic across colour, typography and composition.

The target audience was young people between 18 and 25 interested in fashion, urban culture, the latest trends and oversized clothing, who see the way they dress as a form of self-expression. Based on this profile, messages and formats were designed to create identification with Category Five Brand’s lifestyle and to reinforce its positioning within urban fashion.

Community engagement was driven through challenges, polls and contests on social media, helping to increase organic reach and strengthen the connection between the brand and its audience. The campaign aimed to consolidate a fresh and contemporary image of Category Five Brand, aligned with current streetwear references and supported by a consistent visual identity across all graphic, photographic, animated and audiovisual pieces.
