Category Five Brand – Digital campaign

A content and social campaign for Category Five Brand that combines video, photography, graphics and logo animations to connect with a young streetwear audience on Instagram and TikTok.

Client Category Five Brand
Year 2023
My role Art direction, graphic design & digital content
Domain Streetwear & urban fashion
01 · Campaign

Three videos to bring the brand’s summer universe to life

The campaign is built around three short videos that explore different summer activities connected to the brand’s world: days at the beach, motorbike rides and urban skate events. Each piece was designed to highlight specific garments from the new collection and show how they work in real contexts, aligned with the audience’s lifestyle.

In parallel, we developed a digital campaign focused on Instagram and TikTok, combining photographic content, graphic pieces and logo animations. The publishing plan integrated Stories, Reels and grid posts that worked both as a visual lookbook and as a showcase of the brand’s identity, keeping colour, typography and composition consistent throughout.

The main target was 18–25 year olds interested in fashion, urban culture, the latest trends and oversized clothing, who see the way they dress as a form of self‑expression. From this profile, we defined messages and formats to create identification with Category Five Brand’s lifestyle and reinforce its positioning within urban fashion.

Community engagement was encouraged through social media challenges, polls and giveaways, which helped to increase organic reach and strengthen the relationship between the brand and its audience. The campaign aimed to consolidate a fresh, contemporary image of Category Five Brand, aligned with current streetwear references and supported by a consistent visual identity across graphic, photographic, animated and audiovisual assets.
