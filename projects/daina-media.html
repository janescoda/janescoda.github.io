01 · Project

From academic framework to a real digital agency

Daina Media is a digital marketing agency created as a Master’s project with a fully applied approach: taking theory and testing it against a realistic case. The work starts with an analysis of the economic context, the digital sector and the needs of SMEs and professionals, in order to define an agency specialised in helping small businesses transition into the online environment.

Using tools such as SWOT, benchmarking and the Business Model Canvas, the project defines customer segments, value proposition, channels and cost structure with a clear positioning: a close, agile and creative agency aimed at small brands that value authenticity and design. On the branding side, the work covers purpose, mission, vision and personality, building the “Daina” brand (deer in Catalan) as a symbol of agility and elegance, reinforced by a minimal visual identity in black, white and blue, sans‑serif typefaces and a clean, digital aesthetic.

One of the key outputs is the creation of the website dainamedia.com, developed in WordPress on its own domain and hosting. The site is the central hub of the brand, with “About”, “Services”, “Values” and “Contact” sections in three languages (Spanish, Catalan and English), all aligned with the defined visual identity. The launch is completed with profiles on LinkedIn, Instagram and Facebook, each with tailored tone and visual line, plus an initial content calendar designed to introduce the brand, explain services, show the visual style and connect with entrepreneurs and small businesses.

The project doesn’t stop at a theoretical business plan: it builds a brand, a website and real communication assets that could be used by an actual agency.
02 · Social

Instagram grid to introduce the brand

The Instagram content works as a visual introduction to Daina Media: it presents the identity, explains the main services and reinforces the positioning as a close, honest partner for small brands. The grid mixes typographic pieces, simple layouts and brand photography to keep a consistent, recognisable look.

Selection of Instagram posts used to present the Daina Media identity, communicate services, reinforce the brand positioning and speak directly to SMEs and independent professionals.

03 · Website

dainamedia.com as the centre of the brand

The website was designed as the main reference point for the brand: a place where visitors can quickly understand what Daina Media does, how it works and what it stands for. The structure keeps navigation simple and focuses on copy clarity, with sections dedicated to who they are, what they offer and how to get in touch.

Screens from dainamedia.com: homepage with value proposition, “About” and “Services” pages, a “Values” section that supports the positioning, a clear contact page and responsive layouts optimised for mobile devices.
