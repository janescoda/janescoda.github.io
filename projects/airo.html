Airo

University project

A universal public transport card and digital service designed to reward sustainable mobility habits in large cities.

Overview

Airo was a university entrepreneurship and innovation proposal created with a team of classmates. The project envisioned a universal transport card for train, tram, bus and metro, designed to encourage the use of public transport through a reward system and to promote a more sustainable and active lifestyle.

Client University project
Year 2023
Sector Mobility
Role Visual design & content
Context

Making public transport more attractive

Encouraging people to swap the private car for a unified, connected public transport experience.

The main goal was to reduce car usage and CO₂ emissions in large cities while ensuring public transport remained affordable, comfortable, safe and efficient. To orchestrate the experience, the concept included both a website and a mobile app where users could check routes, benefits and redeem the rewards generated by their everyday mobility.

The proposal also incorporated a fully digital card option to minimise the consumption of physical materials and align the service with environmental sustainability principles. Bringing every transport mode together under a single card simplified the user experience and strengthened the idea of a more connected, intelligent and environmentally friendly urban mobility ecosystem.
